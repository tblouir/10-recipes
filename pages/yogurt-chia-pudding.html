Yogurt-Chia Pudding

  • 2 cups plain whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup, plus more agave
  • Top with cocoa nibs, sunflower seeds, and/or toasted coconut (optional)

Mix 2 cups plain whole-milk yogurt, 1 1/2 tablespoons chia seeds, and 1 tablespoon agave syrup in a large bowl; cover and chill at least 12 hours.

Serve, drizzled with more agave, if desired, and topped with cocoa nibs, sunflower seeds, and/or toasted coconut.
